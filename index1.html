Sampling Strategy

Stratified Multistage Sampling:

  • Primary units: Districts in Jharkhand
  • Secondary units: Villages within selected districts
  • Final units: Individual farmers
  • Stratification by: Farm size, crop type, and geographic region

Data Collection Tools

  • Digital Questionnaire: Tablet-based survey with GPS coordinates
  • Drone Photography: High-resolution aerial images with geo-tagging
  • Soil Sampling Kit: Portable pH meters and testing equipment
  • Weather Sensors: Micro-climate data recording devices

Quality Assurance

  • 10% random validation surveys
  • Real-time data validation algorithms
  • Expert verification of technical responses
  • Cross-reference with government databases
