📊 Comprehensive Farmer Survey – Details

Goal: Build a high-quality dataset that supports precision advisory, sustainability planning, and market access.

Components

  • Sampling: Stratified multistage sampling across villages and farm sizes.
  • Instruments: Digital forms with GPS, media capture, and offline sync.
  • Quality Control: Supervisor back-checks, logic checks, and spot audits.
  • Ethics: Consent forms, anonymization, and secure storage.

Deliverables

  • Clean master dataset (CSV + metadata dictionary)
  • Interactive dashboard (KPIs, maps, trends)
  • Insights report with recommendations

🤝 KYFKYC E-Marketplace – Details

The KYFKYC platform directly connects producers and consumers with transparent provenance and fair pricing.

Key Features

  • Daily listings, live availability, and demand forecasting
  • Farmer profile pages with ratings and certifications
  • Route optimization for morning deliveries
  • Traceability ledger for lot-level history
  • Wallets, UPI, and cash-on-delivery options

🌳 Agroforestry Integration – Details

Landscape-scale suitability modeling for silvipastoral and agrosilvopastoral systems.

What We Assess

  • Soil type, slope, and moisture regimes
  • Windbreak and shade requirements
  • Intercrop compatibility and seasonal calendars
  • Livestock integration and fodder balance

🔬 ML-Powered Insights – Details

Predictive models for disease, yield, and price signals, tailored to farm context.

Pipeline

  • Data ingestion → validation → feature engineering
  • Model training with cross-validation and drift monitoring
  • Explainable outputs (feature importance, SHAP-style summaries)
  • Advisory generation with confidence levels
